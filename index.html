Who We Are

Alley Cat Allies is a dedicated non-profit organization committed to improving the lives of stray and feral cats through humane and effective methods. Our primary focus is on implementing and promoting Trap-Neuter-Return (TNR) programs as a compassionate approach to managing feral cat populations. Through TNR, we work to control the cat population, reduce the number of homeless kittens, and enhance the overall well-being of feral cats. Additionally, we engage in advocacy efforts, striving to create and support policies that ensure the humane treatment of cats while providing valuable resources and education to communities, animal control agencies, and individuals. Join us in our mission to create a world where all cats are treated with kindness and compassion.

Our Achievements

Through our dedicated efforts, we've successfully spayed and neutered over 1,000 cats, contributing to the reduction of stray populations and promoting a healthier environment for both feline companions and communities.

Our advocacy has reached and engaged with 50+ communities, fostering a sense of responsibility and compassion towards stray cats. By promoting Trap-Neuter-Return, we've cultivated a network of caring individuals committed to improving the lives of feral feline populations..

Our advocacy has conducted 100+ educational outreach sessions, equipping communities with the knowledge and tools to implement effective Trap-Neuter-Return programs. This achievement reflects our commitment to creating sustainable solutions for the welfare of stray and feral cats.
