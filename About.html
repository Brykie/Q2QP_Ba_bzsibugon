Background

Founded in 1990, Alley Cat Allies is a pioneering non-profit organization dedicated to the protection and well-being of stray and feral cats. As a trailblazer in the field of feline advocacy, our organization has been at the forefront of promoting humane and non-lethal methods for managing cat populations. Alley Cat Allies is a staunch advocate for the Trap-Neuter-Return (TNR) approach, which emphasizes the humane trapping, sterilization, and return of feral cats to their communities. The organization is deeply engaged in public education and awareness campaigns, seeking to change perceptions and foster compassion for outdoor cats. Additionally, we also provide valuable resources and support for communities looking to implement effective and humane strategies for the care of feral cat colonies. Through our dedication, We have played a pivotal role in reshaping policies and attitudes towards the welfare of community cats, creating a positive impact on feline lives across the nation.

Goals

Mission

We are dedicated to advocating for the well-being of cats through humane and sustainable solutions, emphasizing education, community engagement, and progressive policies to create lasting change

How to Help

Support our mission by donating to fund critical initiatives such as Trap-Neuter-Return programs, public education campaigns, and legislative advocacy. Join our dedicated volunteer team to make a tangible impact on the lives of cats through hands-on care, outreach, or administrative support. Advocate for feline welfare by staying informed about local legislation, engaging with decision-makers, and promoting responsible cat ownership. Help us raise awareness by sharing our mission on social media, attending events, and encouraging others to join the cause. Through your financial support, volunteer efforts, advocacy, and spreading the word, you play a vital role in creating a world where every cat is valued and protected.

Vision

Our vision is a world where every cat, whether stray, feral, or domestic, lives a life free from suffering and enjoys the respect and compassion it deserves
